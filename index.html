WHAT WE DELIVER

Comprehensive Services

A complete suite of medical billing and practice management solutions designed to maximize revenue and minimize administrative burden.

Provider Enrollment & Credentialing

We maintain a complete database for provider enrollment, government contracts, insurance carriers, and hospital credentialing. All applications prepared, documents scanned and routed for signature.

Fast turnaround • Full tracking

Medical Billing & Coding

Accurate, timely claim submission and expert coding guidance. Our certified team ensures compliance with current regulations while optimizing reimbursement for every specialty we serve.

Coding audits included

Electronic Billing & Payments

Direct electronic claims submission and funds transfer (EFT) from Medicare, Blue Cross, United Healthcare, Medi-Cal, Blue Shield, Aetna and many other payers. Faster payments, fewer errors.

Real-time payer connectivity

Collections & Appeals

Aggressive yet professional follow-up including appeals, dedicated collection calls, and on-site payer visits ("road trips") when necessary. Strong established relationships deliver superior results.

"We get paid when you get paid"

Customized Financial Reporting

Detailed tracking and reporting through our advanced billing system. Request the exact reports that matter most to your practice — from AR aging to payer performance and productivity metrics.

Actionable insights, on demand

Patient Engagement

Automated appointment confirmation calls and statements. We help reduce no-shows and improve patient collections while maintaining a professional and courteous patient experience.

Improved cash flow & scheduling

Financial Data Management (FDM) is a full-service medical billing and practice management company offering a complete range of tailored services for physicians and their practices.

We have delivered reliable results for over three decades. Our team stays current on every compliance and legislative development so your practice stays protected and optimized.

We believe in a true team approach. You gain direct access to specialists in billing, coding, collections, and IT — all working together on your behalf.

Team of Experts

Dedicated account managers, certified coders, experienced collectors, and IT professionals who understand the full revenue cycle.

Two Strategic Offices

Santa Ana, California and Coeur d’Alene, Idaho. Local presence with national payer expertise.
